Directions (Public Transit)

Guelph bus information can be found at the Guelph Transit website. Adult/student fare is $3.00 in exact cash.

From Guelph Central Station
    The following routes all stop on campus at University Centre bus loop.
        5 Gordon (Platform 4)
        2A West Loop Clockwise (Platform 1)
        3B East Loop Counter Clockwise (Platform 6)

From VIA Rail Terminal, GO Bus Terminal
    Follow Carden Street to Guelph Central Station.

From Greyhound Bus Terminal (temporary terminal on Wyndham Street at Fountain Street)
    1. Turn left and walk north up Wyndham Street S.
    2. Turn right on Carden Street to Guelph Central Station.

Parking

Parking is included with a residence room booking, for those wishing to stay on campus.
Otherwise, parking Thursday evening (after 5 pm) and Saturday is free in lot P31.
Friday: Parking is available in P14 and P15. We will provide parking passes free of charge when you check in Thursday evening. Be sure the pass is clearly visible in your windshield.


Directions (Driving)

From Highway 401
    1. Take exit 295 to Highway 6 N/Hanlon Parkway.
    2. Turn right on Stone Road W.
    3. Turn left on Gordon St.
    4. Take immediate right on South Ring Rd.
    Thursday and Saturday: Use P31 on the right.
    Friday: Turn left on East Ring Rd. P14 and P15 are on the right.

From Highway 7 (Kitchener, Waterloo)
    1. Continue onto Woodlawn Rd W.
    2. Turn right onto Highway 6/Hanlon Parkway.
    3. Turn left on Stone Rd.
    4. Turn left on Gordon St.
    4. Take immediate right on South Ring Rd.
    Thursday and Saturday: Use P31 on the right.
    Friday: Turn left on East Ring Rd. P14 and P15 are on the right.

From Highway 6 South (Hamilton)
    1. Continue onto Highway 6 S/Brock Road S.
    2. Continue onto Brock Rd N, Gordon St.
    3. Turn right on Stone Road E.
    Thursday and Saturday: Use P31 on the right.
    Friday: Turn left on East Ring Rd. P14 and P15 are on the right.
