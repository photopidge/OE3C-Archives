This year, the University of Guelph is proud to host the 44th annual OE3C. The University boasts an array of food choices on campus, as well as the beautiful Arboretum, with over 9km of trails. We encourage you to explore Guelph’s charming downtown, only a short bus ride from campus, which prides itself on its local food and green spaces.

Public Transit

Guelph bus information can be found at the Guelph Transit website. Adult/student fare is $3.00 in exact cash.
