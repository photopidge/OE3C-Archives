Main Page Content

The Ontario Ecology, Ethology, and Evolution Colloquium (OE3C) is an annual provincial conference hosted by graduate students across various universities in Ontario. Every year, for over 40 years, undergraduates, graduates, post-docs, and faculty in Ontario have attended this conference to present their research, learn about new research from others, and network with fellow academics. OE3C is a great way for students to receive supportive and constructive feedback about their research in an intimate academic environment, and for faculty to recruit, and interact with, prospective students. Researchers at all levels of their careers are welcome to submit poster and talk presentations about diverse topics, such as conservation, evolution, behaviour, environmental science, genetics, and psychology.

This year, we are proud to announce that OE3C 2014 will be hosted by the University of Guelph. We invite undergraduates, graduates, post-docs, and faculty across Ontario to share their new research in May 2014!
